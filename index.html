About

Hello! My name is Carol Carter, I am a web developer based in Atlanta, GA. My background is working as a studio manager for a woodworking artist and an artisanal soap maker. The process of helping artists bridge the gap between the craft they have a passion for and the necessity of running the business side of their operation became a passion of mine. I quickly came to understand the huge power a strong web presence has in helping business grow and thrive. With this as my motivation I set out to learn how to build a website! Never did I dream that through my pursuit I would learn that I actually loved programming and building not just a “website” but functional web applications! I studied Web Dev at Gwinnett Technical College in Lawrenceville GA for 18 months and am currently enrolled in Digital Crafts Immersive Full Stack Development program. I will graduate in July 2021 and am seeking a position in the Atlanta metro area (I am open to remote opportunities) that will provide an environment for growth and the ability to enrich people's lives!

When I’m not behind a computer screen I enjoy getting outside to hike and camp, rummaging through treasures at a good estate sale and playing with my three cats! If you are interested in working together or have any questions for me I’d love to hear from you!
